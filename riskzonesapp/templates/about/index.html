CityZones Web is a web interface for the RiskZones algorithm written by Ph.D candidate João Paulo Just Peixoto under supervision of Prof. Daniel G. Costa, Ph.D (FEUP) and Prof. Washington de Jesus Sant'Anna da Franca Rocha, Ph.D (UEFS). It also has a research intership at FEUP, with supersion of Prof. Paulo Portugal, Ph.D.

The web interface of the CityZones tool classifies zones in an AoI (Area of Interest) according to its mitigation levels, i.e, how hard is to mitigate an emergency after the occurence of an event in that zone. The zones are classified in three levels: 1 (green), 2 (yellow) and 3 (red), being red the highest risk and green the lowest. It is based on the premise that the higher the risk level, the harder is to sucessful perform a mitigation action in that zone (rescue, fire figthing, crisis control, etc).
