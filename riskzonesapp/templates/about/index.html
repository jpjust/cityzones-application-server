CityZones Web is a web interface for riskzones algorithm written by Ph.D candidate João Paulo Just Peixoto under supervision of Prof. Daniel G. Costa, Ph.D (FEUP/UEFS) and Prof. Washington de Jesus Sant'Anna da Franca Rocha, Ph.D (UEFS). The work at FEUP is being supervised by Prof. Paulo Portugal, Ph.D.

This algorithm classifies zones in an AoI (Area of Interest) according to its mitigation levels, i.e, how hard is to mitigate an emergency after the occurence of an event in that zone. The zones are classified in three levels: 1 (green), 2 (yellow) and 3 (red). The higher the level, the hardest is to start a mitigation process in that zone.

To have a full understanding of this work, please read the papers published on it:

  • Optimizing the deployment of multi-sensors emergencies detection units based on the presence of response centers in smart cities

This work has been conducted at FEUP/University of Porto during a Doctoral Exchange Program funded by CAPES.
